Submitting feedback on Ambulant Player

If you encounter a bug in the player please report it to us. The best way to report a bug is through the SourceForge bug tracker, you will find a link Bug Reports to it on the AmbulantPlayer homepage. Alternatively you can mail your bug report to Ambulant-feedback@ambulantplayer.org.

If the bug is repeatable you can help us a lot by providing the debugging output, in the following way:

  • Set Log level to debug in the preferences.
  • Stop and restart the player.
  • Repeat the steps to demonstrate the bug.
  • Show the Logging window, and copy the contents into a file.
  • Tell us about your system: operating system and version, etc.

In addition information such as your operating system and version, and possibly the document you were trying to play back are helpful.
