Ambulant Player Introduction

Ambulant Player is an Open Source player for SMIL 2.0 documents (The full licensing details can be found in the developer section of this help file). It is intended primarily for the research community, and not as a replacement for commercial SMIL players such as the RealPlayer or QuickTime: while the performance is at least on par with such players (in our not so humble opinion:-) the Ambulant Player does not provide support for the wide range of media encoding formats that such players support.
